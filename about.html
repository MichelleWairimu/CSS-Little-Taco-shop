The Little taco shop was founded by Miguel el Roso in the year 1925 after moving to Kenya with his family. Miguel wanted to unite the different tribes in Kenya with his food and sort to inpire unity among the natives. They run the Little Taco shop with his wife Maria Esperanza. In order to bring the natives together Miguel started a tradition called Undugu Night where natives from different tribes came to show case more about their culture. Ever since then the tradition has been in place every Thursday night when people come to perform art, through dancing, singing, poetry and so much more. Miguel believed that to end a war feed the man then entertain his soul.

Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
